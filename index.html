About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Leon, currently a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Bei Yu. Prior to that, I had the honor to be with HKU-EEE and PKU-CECA, advised by Dr. Ngai Wong and Prof. Guojie Luo, respectively. I also received my B.S. degree in Computer Science and Techonology from Peking University.

My current research enthusiasm lies in solving combinatorial optimization problems, especially those with electronic design automation (EDA) applications, via machine learning (ML) methods. I also worked on application-driven heterougenous accelerator design, and share general (yet pending) intersts in tensor applications, natural language processing (NLP), emerging computing paradigms (e.g. Hyperdimensional Computing), and software hardware co-design methodologies (e.g. Overlay).

With effervescence, I am delighted to be fancied as a barista/lensman/guitarist/whatever connoisseur etc. Nevertheless, "the successful warrior is the average man, with laser-like focus".
