About

I am currently a Research Assistant in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE), The University of Hong Kong, supervised by Dr. Ngai Wong.
Prior to that, I had been with Center for Energy-efficient Computing and Applications (CECA), Peking University for over three years, where Prof. Guojie Luo was my supervisor. I also received my B.S degree in Computer Science and Techonology from Peking University.

My current research interests include tensor applications and graph learning.
I also worked on application-driven heterougenous accelerator design, and share general interests on electronic design automation (EDA), natural language processing (NLP), machine learning (ML), software hardware co-design methodology (e.g. Overlay), and program synthesis.

I am a HUGE coffee fan, enjoying coffee tasting and brewing.
