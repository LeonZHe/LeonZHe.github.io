About me

Zhuolun (Leon) He is a junior Ph.D student in computer architecture at Peking University. He is a member of Center for Energy-efficient Computing and Applications advised by Prof. Guojie Luo. His research interest includes application-driven heterogenous accelerator design, and natural language processing.

Leon enjoys coffee tasting and brewing.

Publication

  • Zhuolun He and Guojie Luo, "FPGA Acceleration for Computational Glass-Free Displays," 25th ACM/SIGDA International Symposium on Field-Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGA 2017), February 22-24, 2017, Monterey, CA, USA.
