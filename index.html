About

I am currently a Ph.D student in Center for Energy-efficient Computing and Applications, Peking University, supervised by Prof. Guojie Luo.

My research interests include application-driven heterogenous accelerator design, program synthesis, and natural language processing.

I am a HUGE coffee fan, enjoying coffee tasting and brewing.
